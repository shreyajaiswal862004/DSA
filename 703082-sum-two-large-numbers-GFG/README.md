# [Sum Two Large Numbers](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/sum-of-numbers-or-number1219/1?utm=codolio)
## Medium
Given two strings denoting non-negative numbers s1 and s2. Calculate the sum of s1 and s2. 
Examples:
Input: s1 = "25", s2 = "23"
Output: "48"
Explanation: The sum of 25 and 23 is 48.
Input: s1 = "2500", s2 = "23"
Output: "2523"
Explanation: The sum of 2500 and 23 is 2523.
Input: s1 = "2", s2 = "3"
Output: "5"
Explanation: The sum of 2 and 3 is 5.
Constraints:1 &lt;= |s1|, |s2| &lt;= 105
